Please see the instructions on desktop/laptop.

  • Open revelio.ai on your Android phone

    Install the app by clicking Download the app first

    On phone, allow Chrome to "install unknown apps"

    Settings > Apps > Menu > Special access > Install unknown apps

    Find the app icon on phone ➔

  • Check if your monitor supports 60Hz refresh rate

    Use this link to check your monitor's refresh rate

    To run an encoded video click on Run the video below

    Enter the 10-char ID from the app into the prompt

    You'll find it in the app near the top right corner

    On the right is Shrini's id, don't enter that 😄

  • Run the video in fullscreen

    Press the Enter key to toggle to fullscreen

    Then, mouse right-click on video to enable Loop

  • Place the phone around 1/2 m away from screen

    Not too close & not too far

  • The screen or the monitor running the video should be

    comfortably inside the app preview ➔

  • Press the start button and hold really still for 5 seconds

    (holding your breath helps 😄)

  • Web link will open on your phone in a few moments

    Please don't close the app while processing is going on
